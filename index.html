Payment Methods

We accept VISA, MasterCard, American Express, Paypal and Binance

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your reservation through your account on our website or by contacting our customer support team. However, please note that our cancellation policy may vary depending on the rate and room type you have booked.

Check-In and Check-Out

Check-in is typically at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. However, these times may vary based on the hotel's policies.

Room Information

You can request a specific room type or a room with a view when you make your reservation. However, please note that these requests are subject to availability and cannot be guaranteed.

Long Stays

We offer special rates for guests who are staying with us for an extended period of time. Contact us for more information on our long stay rates and availability.

Pet Policy

We have a pet-friendly policy and allow certain types of pets in designated pet-friendly rooms. There may be a fee for pet-friendly rooms and certain restrictions may apply, so please contact us for more information.
